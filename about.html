ABOUT OUR CHURCH

OUR VISION IS TO CONNECT ANYONE ANYWHERE TO A LIFE‑CHANGING RELATIONSHIP WITH JESUS

OUR BELIEFS

We are wholeheartedly committed to building a church that reaches people who don't yet know God. We present the timeless message of the gospel in modern and relevant ways. The message is sacred and not to be altered, but our approach and method is always open to change. We are a Bible-believing church with a theology and beliefs that are the same as hundreds of thousands of churches around the world.

MORE

WHO

We are often asked if Freedom Church is just for Christians. Freedom is a church for everyone on the journey of faith. If you're 'just looking', then this is the right place for you. There is no pressure to believe before you come to our church, and if you have questions, we’re here to help you find answers. Whether you’re taking your first step towards faith, or your one thousandth, there is a place for you at Freedom Church as you go on your journey with God.
