Pastor's Desk
From the Pastor's Desk

“Beloved, I pray that you may prosper in all things and be in health, just as your soul prospers.” - 1Jn. 1:2, NKJV. It is a powerful thing to come to worship on this last Sunday of June and hear Apostle John praying over us the words of the text above. Surely God heard the words - that you may prosper in all things, - that you may be in health and - that you prosper in your soul also. As you worship along with others today, I pray that the grace for this 3-fold prosperity will be yours in the name of Jesus. Amen. Enjoy God!
